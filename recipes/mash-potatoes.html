Mash Potatoes

Description

Potatoes is a easy to make dish, which is very delicious and nutritious.

The preparation time is aproximately of 20 to 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes until soft
  2. Drain water
  3. Add butter and milk
  4. Mash potatoes until desired texture is achieved
  5. Add cheese and salt at your own discretion
