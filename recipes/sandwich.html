Sandwich

Description

Sanwiches make a great snack, very easy to make and carry with you anywhere

The preparation time is aproximately 5 to 8 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Protein (eggs, vegan bologna...)

Steps

  1. Cook your chosen protein
  2. Add all ingredients in between the bread slices
  3. Add condiments according to your preferences
  4. Place sanwiches in the oven or sandwich maker for 2 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
